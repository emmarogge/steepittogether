    This is from a fantastic TED talk by Dame Stephanie Shirley who built an all-women software company on the 60s, that went to be worth $3 billion. Called “Why do ambitious women have flat heads?” please give it a watch if you have time during the Easter holidays! Interestingly, her biggest advice for women entrepreneurs is the same as Sheryl Sandberg’s: You need a supportive partner. I’ve realised that as women, our identities are far more intertwined with our partner’s than vice versa. Just notice how many women proudly describe themselves as “Bride to be”, “Future Mrs”, “Mumtrepreneur”, “Mommy of”, “Wife of” on social media whereas men will only describe themselves with their job titles. Business consultant. CEO. Doctor. This is simply my observation and I hold no judgement for anyone conforming to these norms. But fact is, if you want to be a Future Mrs *and* CEO, then your “Future Mr” had better be someone who 100% believes in your dreams as well 😜!! #ted #inspiration #motivation #workinspiration #lifeinspiration #leanin #empowerment #feminism #success #ambition #lifequotes #instaquote #motivationalquote #inspirationalquote #qotd #quoteoftheday (Photo by Davide Ragusa on Upworthy)
